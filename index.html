Currently a Senior Manager at the Christian Family Service Centre (CFSC), I have extensive frontline and management experience and deeply understand the challenges NGO staff face daily. My unique strength lies in combining hands-on social work experience with professional design skills — offering a one-stop solution from training needs analysis and curriculum design to publication production.
