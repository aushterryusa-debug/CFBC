Why Work With Me?

I have been interviewed on RTHK Radio 1's "Extraordinary People" programme on carer support services, contributed columns to the Hong Kong Economic Times, and participated in cross-sector projects such as the Cancer Carers Handbook. 欣悅坊 — 殘疾人士地區支援中心（觀塘東）

Cheerful Place — District Support Centre for Persons with Disabilities (Kwun Tong East)

馬韻瑜現時管理的旗艦服務單位。欣悅坊由社會福利署資助、基督教家庭服務中心營運，為觀塘東區（翠屏、寶達、秀茂坪、藍田、油塘、鯉魚門）殘疾人士及其照顧者提供一站式社區支援服務。中心以跨專業團隊模式運作，提供 13 類服務。

Mary Ma's flagship service unit. Cheerful Place is funded by the Social Welfare Department and operated by CFSC, providing one-stop community support for persons with disabilities and their carers across Kwun Tong East (Tsui Ping, Po Tat, Sau Mau Ping, Lam Tin, Yau Tong, Lei Yue Mun). The centre operates with a multidisciplinary team offering 13 service categories.

  • 13 類服務：個人/小組訓練、社交康樂、日間照顧、暫託、家居支援、照顧者支援、專職治療（物理/職業/言語）、個案管理、臨床心理、社區教育、離校生支援等
  • 13 service types: individual/group training, social recreation, day care, respite, home support, carer support, allied health therapy (physio/OT/speech), case management, clinical psychology, community education, school leaver support
  • 日間暫託 $5/小時 | 日間照顧連午膳 $61/天 | 專職治療家訪 $52/節 | 會員年費 $21
  • Day respite $5/hr | Day care with lunch $61/day | Allied health home visit $52/session | Annual membership $21
  • 出版季刊通訊（2022年至今共 18 期），記錄中心活動、服務成果及社區資源
  • Publishes quarterly newsletters (18 issues since 2022), documenting activities, service outcomes, and community resources
  • 2024年4月新增「離校生支援隊」，支援特殊學校畢業生融入社區
  • Launched "School Leaver Support Team" in April 2024 for special school graduates transitioning to community life

照顧者快線 CarerExpress

Carer Express Service

以「L.I.N.K.」理念統籌的照顧者外展服務，透過流動宣傳車及電話快線，走訪觀塘、黃大仙及將軍澳，及早識別照顧者需要。啟動禮獲社會福利署署長杜永恒先生 JP 主禮，服務至今超過 7,000 人次。

A carer outreach service built on the "L.I.N.K." framework, using mobile outreach vans and a telephone hotline across Kwun Tong, Wong Tai Sin, and Tseung Kwan O for early identification of carer needs. Launch ceremony officiated by SWD Director Mr. Gordon Leung, JP. Over 7,000 service contacts to date.

  • L — Life Unfolded：真人圖書館，照顧者分享真實故事
  • L — Life Unfolded: Real-life library sharing stories from fellow caregivers
  • I — Innovative Technologies：AI 智能家居裝置，減輕照顧負擔
  • I — Innovative Technologies: AI smart home devices reducing caregiving burden
  • N — Nurturing Wellness：運動及精神健康支援小組
  • N — Nurturing Wellness: Exercise and mental health support groups
  • K — Kickstart Knowledge：實用照顧技巧及數碼共融培訓
  • K — Kickstart Knowledge: Practical caregiving skills and digital inclusion
  • 服務對象：東九龍潛在需要照顧者、在職照顧者、高壓照顧者、體弱照顧者
  • Target groups: potential carers in East Kowloon, working carers, highly stressed carers, frail carers

  • Free service | Hotline: 2997 8233 (office hours)
