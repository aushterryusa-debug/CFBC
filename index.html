Why Work With Me?

我現職基督教家庭服務中心（CFSC）殘疾人士綜合服務 — 日間照顧及地區支援高級經理，擁有超過十五年社福界前線及管理經驗。由健康推廣主任到高級經理，我深入了解 NGO 各層面的運作需要。

Currently Senior Manager of Integrated Services for Persons with Disabilities — Day Care & District Support at the Christian Family Service Centre (CFSC), I have over 15 years of frontline and management experience in the social welfare sector.

我曾獲邀接受香港電台第一台《非常人物生活雜誌》節目訪問，分享照顧者支援服務經驗；亦曾在《香港經濟日報》撰寫專欄，並參與癌症病人照顧者手冊等跨機構協作項目。我的服務特色在於結合社福實務經驗與專業設計能力，從培訓需要分析、課程設計到刊物製作，提供一站式支援。

I have been interviewed on RTHK Radio 1's "Extraordinary People" programme on carer support services, contributed columns to the Hong Kong Economic Times, and participated in cross-sector projects such as the Cancer Carers Handbook. My strength lies in combining hands-on social work experience with professional design skills — offering one-stop support from training needs analysis to publication production.

服務管理 Management

照顧者快線 CarerExpress

Carer Express Service

以「L.I.N.K.」理念統籌「照顧者快線」服務，流動車走訪觀塘、黃大仙及將軍澳，配合電話快線及早識別照顧者需要。啟動禮獲社會福利署署長杜永恒先生 JP 主禮，服務至今超過 7,000 人次。

Led the "Carer Express" programme based on the "L.I.N.K." concept — a mobile outreach van covering Kwun Tong, Wong Tai Sin, and Tseung Kwan O with telephone support for early carer identification. Launch ceremony officiated by SWD Director. Over 7,000 service contacts to date.
